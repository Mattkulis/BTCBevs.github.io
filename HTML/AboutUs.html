At Face Value, we are simply a youth-oriented beverage company, offering a bitcoin-themed refreshment and free bitcoin in each bottle. We aim to meet the demand of consumers interested in new fruity, carbonated soda/pop, riding both the tailwinds of the last crypto-market cycle as well as the headwinds of the next.

Ultimately, Bitcoin Beverages is more than just a beverage company—it's a catalyst for financial literacy and individual empowerment, challenging conventional wisdom and paving the way for a new generation of economically savvy individuals. Through education and innovation, we aim to reshape the way people understand and interact with finance, one sip at a time.

Based in Chicago, IL, our line of sweet and citrusy carbonated beverages offers a premium experience that indulges the taste buds while providing a simple and digestible gateway to understanding the complexities of economics and finance, specifically centered around Bitcoin and Bitcoin Adoption.

While competitors like Calipso, Jarritos, and Herbert’s Lemonade exist in the beverage market, our unique proposition of combining indulgence with the technological zeitgeist distinguishes us from the pack.

Our business model is straightforward: we sell beverages and give away bitcoin in each drink, supplementing product revenue with ad revenue from partnered ads on the bottle. We distribute our beverages in stores and directly to consumers.
